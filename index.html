About Me

I am a senior at Depaul University studying to get my Bachelor of Science, majoring in Software Development and minoring in Game Design. Currently, I am working as a Cybernavigator at the Chicago Public Library, providing patrons with curated computer classes and helping them breach the digital divide in underprivileged communities. I also help patrons with hardware and software issues they might be having with their devices.

My academic journey has given me a broad and robust foundation, teaching me how to develop code as well as express myself creatively through games. I have learned about the principles of game design, how to create characters and stories, and how to use code to bring these elements to life. I have also learned about the business side of game development, such as how to market and sell games. My academic journey has prepared me for a career in game development, and I am excited to use my skills to create games that people will enjoy.
